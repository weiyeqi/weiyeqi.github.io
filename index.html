I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois Chicago (UIC), working under the supervision of Dr. Natasha Devroye.

My research focuses on physical unclonable function and deep-learned error-correcting codes.

I obtained my master's degree from UIC in May 2018, and my bachelor's degree from Northeastern University, China in June 2017.
